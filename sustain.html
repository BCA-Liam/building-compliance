🌱 Sustainability Consulting
Sustainability consulting helps organisations, developers, and building owners reduce environmental impact, improve energy efficiency, and meet regulatory or voluntary sustainability goals. It bridges the gap between compliance and long-term net-zero strategies.

🔍 What Is It?
Sustainability consultants provide expert advice and technical support to: Design and deliver low-carbon, resource-efficient buildings Navigate planning requirements and building regulations Identify carbon reduction opportunities across construction and operation Integrate sustainable technologies (e.g., renewables, efficient systems)

🧰 Typical Services Include:
Energy and carbon assessments (SAP, SBEM, BREEAM input) Whole-life carbon analysis Sustainable design advice (materials, orientation, thermal performance) Low and zero carbon (LZC) feasibility studies Net-zero strategy development Support with local planning policy and London Plan energy statements

🎯 Why It Matters
Meet Part L and Planning Authority requirements Achieve cost-effective compliance and long-term savings Increase building value and future-proofing
